Homemade Pasta

Restaurant-Worthy Pasta Recipe

Description

Create simple, handmade pasta with only a few ingredients.

    Ingredients
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
    Steps
  1. Mix ingredients in bowl to make the dough.
  2. Knead dough for 10 minutes before dividing into balls.
  3. Use a pasta machine to make pasta of the desired shape.
  4. Boil pasta until ready (about 2 to 5 minutes).
  5. Enjoy!
