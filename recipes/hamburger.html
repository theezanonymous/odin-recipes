Best Hamburger Ever

Awesome Hamburger Recipe

Description

This recipe will help you make the best burger of your life. Gather simple ingredients, mix, and enjoy.

    Ingredients
  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings of Choice
    Steps
  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Form 4 patties by hand.
  3. Grill until juices run and red is no longer red(about 4-5 minutes per side)
  4. Enjoy!
